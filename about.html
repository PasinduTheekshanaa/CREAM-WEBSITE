Our Story

Founded on a simple love for creamy, dreamy desserts.

From Our Kitchen to Your Heart

Welcome to DORA Cream. Our journey began in a small family kitchen, experimenting with flavors and textures to create the perfect scoop. We believe that ice cream isn't just a treat; it's a moment of joy, a celebration, and a comfort.

Every batch of DORA Cream is churned with care, using only the freshest milk, real fruits, and premium chocolates. We never compromise on quality because we know you can taste the difference.

Today, we continue to innovate with new, exciting flavors while honoring the classics that everyone loves. Thank you for being part of our sweet journey.
